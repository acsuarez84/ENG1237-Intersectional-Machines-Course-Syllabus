Section 5

AI Bias Supplemental Readings

Fourteen case studies of algorithmic bias to journal and reflect on.

Here you have a list of 14 AI bias supplemental readings for which you will journal and reflect on in preparation for your short and final assignments. These readings will supplement the core reading materials in section 4. Your reflections and your argument must connect to the theories of intersectionality, feminism, and critical making.

Hiring & Employment Algorithms

Amazon’s Biased Hiring Algorithm (2018) — gender bias. “Why Amazon’s Automated Hiring Tool Discriminated Against Women.” ACLU.

Bloomberg’s ChatGPT / GPT hiring-bias test (2024). Nicoletti, Leonardo, and Dina Bass. “OpenAI’s GPT is a Recruiter’s Dream Tool. Tests Show There’s Racial Bias.” Bloomberg, 2024.
